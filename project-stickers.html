Leeward CC Marketing Sticker Campaign

Print Design | Campus Promotion | Spring 2024

Project Overview

I created a set of custom promotional stickers for the Leeward Community College Marketing Department. These stickers were designed to attract student engagement, boost school pride, and support recruitment efforts during outreach events.

My Role

  • Designed sticker artwork and typography to align with Leeward’s brand
  • Prepared files for print and coordinated vendor production
  • Worked with the Marketing Director to meet branding and deadline goals

Tools Used

Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Canva

Results

The stickers were well-received by students and staff, frequently featured at events and on laptops and water bottles. They helped increase brand visibility in a fun, creative way.
